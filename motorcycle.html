Motorcycle

Yamaha R6

I've always been fascinated with motorcycles since I was a kid. I didn't think I would end up owning one. Some friends from work and I decided to take the MSF (Motorcycle Safety Foundation) course. The 3 day course is offered/mandatory for riding in the Air Force. This was my first time riding; I was ambitious upon finishing the course and bought a Yamaha YZF-R6. I've been riding for over two years now and enjoy riding at Red Rock, Mt. Charleston, and Valley of Fire.
