About Me

Hey! My name is Christopher, and I've been playing guitar for 20 years. I played in local bands for a couple of years until I was hired on to play bass for the band, A Hero A Fake (Victory Records). This gave me insight on the process of recording an album. I got to work with well known names in the industry such as, Joey Sturgis, Cameron Mizell, and Jamie King.
I've been engineering, producing, mixing, and mastering audio since 2009. I continually strive honing my craft in the ever evolving technology of music while focusing on creating a sound that is organic and authentic to the artistry of each client. Whether an aspiring amateur or a seasoned pro, my goal is to provide an exceptional audio service at a price that is compatible with your budget.

I can provide a professional sound in genres including: Metal, Rock, Indie, Country, Rap, Hip Hop, EDM, and more

Other Services:

Drum Programming/Quantizing
Guitar/Bass Guitar Quantizing
Vocal Tuning And Alignment
Reamping (only with clean DI stems)
